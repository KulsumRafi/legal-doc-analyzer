Radius Explorations Ltd.
News Release 2002-16
December 24, 2002


                   $500,000 PRIVATE PLACEMENT

Radius Explorations Ltd. is pleased to announce that it has
arranged a non-brokered private placement of 1,000,000 units at
$0.50 per unit, for proceeds to the Company of $500,000.  Each
unit will consist of one common share and one-half of a warrant.
Each whole warrant will entitle the holder to purchase one
additional common share of the Company at a price of $0.55
exercisable for one year.  A finder's fee is payable in
connection with this financing.

The proceeds will be used for general working capital.

For further information on the Company and its projects, please
call toll free at 1-888-627-9378 or visit our web site at
www.radiusgold.com.
