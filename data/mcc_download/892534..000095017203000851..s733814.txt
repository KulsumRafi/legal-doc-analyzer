                    Certification of CEO and CFO Pursuant to
                             18 U.S.C. Section 1350,
                             as Adopted Pursuant to
                  Section 906 of the Sarbanes-Oxley Act of 2002

In connection with the annual report on Form 20-F of LanOptics Ltd. (the
"Company") for the fiscal year ended December 31, 2001 as filed with the
Securities and Exchange Commission on the date hereof (the "Report"), Meir D.
Burstin, as Chairman of the Board of Directors of the Company, and Dror Israel,
as Director of Finance of the Company, each hereby certifies, pursuant to 18
U.S.C. ss. 1350, as adopted pursuant to ss. 906 of the Sarbanes-Oxley Act of
2002, that, to the best of his knowledge:

         (1)   The Report fully complies with the requirements of
Section 13(a) or 15(d) of the Securities Exchange Act of 1934; and

         (2)   The information contained in the Report fairly presents, in all
material respects, the financial condition and results of operations of the
Company.


/s/ Meir D. Burstin
------------------------------
Name:   Meir D. Burstin
Title:  Chairman
Date:   March 17, 2003


/s/ Dror Israel
------------------------------
Name:   Dror Israel
Title:  Director of Finance
Date:   March 17, 2003

This certification accompanies the Report pursuant to ss. 906 of the
Sarbanes-Oxley Act of 2002 and shall not, except to the extent required by the
Sarbanes-Oxley Act of 2002, be deemed filed by the Company for purposes of ss.18
of the Securities Exchange Act of 1934, as amended.
