EXHIBIT 2 - Certificates pursuant to Section 906 of the Sarbanes-Oxley Act
of 2002

                                 Certifications

In connection with the annual report of Axcan Pharma Inc.(the "Company") filed
under cover of a Form 40-F for the period ended September 30, 2002 as filed with
the Securities and Exchange Commission on the date hereof (the "Report"), I,
Leon F. Gosselin, Chairman of the Board, President and Chief Executive Officer
of the Company, certify that:

1.   The Report fully complies with the requirements of Section 13(a) or 15(d)
     of the Securities Exchange Act of 1934; and

2.   The information contained in the Report fairly presents, in all material
     respects, the financial condition and results of operations of the Company.
