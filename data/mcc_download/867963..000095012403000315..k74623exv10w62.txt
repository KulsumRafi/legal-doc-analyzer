This letter is to inform you that Standard Federal Bank has agreed to waive,
subject to receipt of a $5,000.00 "waiver fee", the minimum net worth covenant,
provided for in Section III.A.2 of the Business Loan Agreement, as of December
31, 2002.  The Bank hereby also agrees to modify the requirement to "not less
than $6 million" as of each of the fiscal quarters ending on or after March 31,
2003.
