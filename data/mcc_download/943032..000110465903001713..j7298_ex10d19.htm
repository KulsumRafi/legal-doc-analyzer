AMENDMENT TO LOAN AGREEMENT AND NOTE

 

This amendment (the “Amendment”), dated as of the date specified below, is by and between the borrower (the “Borrower”) and the bank (the “Bank”) identified below.

 

RECITALS

 

A. The Borrower and the Bank have executed a Loan Agreement (the “Agreement”) dated MARCH 15, 2002 and the Borrower has executed a Note (the “Note”), dated MARCH 15, 2002, either or both which may have been amended and replaced from time to time, and the Borrower (and if applicable, certain third parties) have executed the collateral documents which may or may not be identified in the Agreement and certain other related documents (collectively the “Loan Documents”), setting forth the terms and conditions upon which the Borrower may obtain loans from the Bank from time to time in the original amount of $ 3,500,000.00, as may be amended from time to time.

 

B. The Borrower has requested that the Bank permit certain modifications to the Agreement and Note as described below.

 

C. The Bank has agreed to such modifications, but only upon the terms and conditions outlined in this Amendment.
