                Re: IMMEDIATE REPORT


The Industrial Development Bank of Israel, Ltd. ("the Bank") requests to advise
that it was served today with the derivative suit which was filed by Lahava
Chatamim Ltd. against eleven officers of the Bank and against the Bank itself,
and about which reports were published in the newspapers during the last few
days.

The amount of the suit is 409.5 million NIS and according to the claim in the
suit this amount represents the damage which the sued officers caused the Bank
by their negligence regarding credit given to various borrowers.
