                                 OMNICARE, INC.

                             1998 Long-Term Employee
                           Incentive Plan (the "Plan")

                             Statement of Amendment

"The aggregate number of shares of Common Stock that may be issued under the
Plan shall not exceed 6,300,000 shares."
