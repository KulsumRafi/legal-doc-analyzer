                            CERTIFICATE OF AMENDMENT
                                     OF THE
                          CERTIFICATE OF INCORPORATION
                                       OF
                        INTERVEST CORPORATION OF NEW YORK

                Under Section 805 of the Business Corporation Law


     The undersigned,  being the Vice President of INTERVEST  CORPORATION OF NEW
YORK (the "Corporation"), hereby certifies:

     1. The name of the  Corporation  is INTERVEST  CORPORATION OF NEW YORK. The
name under which it was formed is MULTIVEST CORPORATION OF NEW YORK.

     2. The Certificate of Incorporation was filed by the Department of State on
April 15, 1987.

     3. The Certificate of  Incorporation,  as now in full force and effect,  is
hereby  amended to effect the  following  change as authorized by Section 801 of
the Business Corporation Law:

     (a)  To  change  the  name  of  the   Corporation  to  INTERVEST   MORTGAGE
     CORPORATION.   The  Paragraph   designated   "1"  in  the   Certificate  of
     Incorporation is hereby amended to read in its entirety as follows:

          1. The name of the corporation is INTERVEST MORTGAGE CORPORATION.


     4. This  Amendment to the  Certificate of  Incorporation  was authorized by
vote at a meeting of the Board of Directors  followed by the written  consent of
the Sole Shareholder.

     IN WITNESS  WHEREOF,  I have signed this Certificate of Amendment this 22nd
day of August, 2002.


                                         ---------------------------------------
                                         Lawrence G. Bergman, Vice President
