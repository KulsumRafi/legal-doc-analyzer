Pursuant to Section 906 of the Sarbanes-Oxley Act of 2002

In connection with the Annual Report of Placer Dome Inc. (the “Company”) on Form 40-F for the year ended December 31, 2002, as filed with the Securities and Exchange Commission on the date hereof (the “Report”), Jay K.      (2)  The information contained in the Report fairly presents, in all material respects, the financial condition and result of operations of the Company.
