WESTPAC BANKING CORPORATION —   PAID UP CAPITAL
  —   QUOTED CAPITAL

A total of 56,000 new fully paid shares were allotted today following the exercise of options pursuant to Westpac's Senior Officers' Share Purchase Scheme (SOSPS). Exercise prices for these options range from $9.53 (51,000) to $9.56 (5,000).

Paid-up Capital

Previously:   1,767,237,974 ordinary shares each fully paid.

Now:
 
1,767,293,974 (increase of 56,000) ordinary shares each fully paid.

Quoted Capital

Quoted capital is 1,766,870,974 (increase of 51,000) fully paid ordinary shares.

Since the record date of 27 November 2002, 423,000 fully paid ordinary shares (increase of 5,000) have been allotted upon exercise of options. These shares are ex-dividend until following next record date in June 2003 and listing will be sought shortly after that date.

Yours faithfully


Julie Thorburn
