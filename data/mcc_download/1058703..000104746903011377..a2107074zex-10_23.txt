     This Amendment No. 2 (this "AMENDMENT") to the Allegiance Telecom, Inc.
Restricted Stock Agreement dated June 7, 2002 as amended on November 5, 2002
(the "ORIGINAL AGREEMENT") is being signed as of February 24, 2003, by and
between Allegiance Telecom, Inc., a Delaware corporation and the person signing

to such terms in the Original Agreement.
