INDEMNIFICATION AGREEMENT

        THIS INDEMNIFICATION AGREEMENT (the “Agreement”) is made as of February 10th, 2003, by and among InfoShowerX Co., Ltd., a corporation organized and existing under the laws of Japan (“InfoShowerX”); iAccele Co., Ltd., a corporation organized and existing under the laws of Japan (“iAccele”); and IA Global, Inc., a corporation organized and existing under the laws of the State of Delaware (“IAG”).

P r e l i m i n a r y S t a t e m e n t s

    A.        IAG has agreed with iAccele to invest One Hundred Million Japanese Yen (¥100,000,000) in iAccele (the “Investment”), and to receive, in consideration therefor, Two Thousand (2,000) shares of iAccele’s Common Stock, each with a face value of Fifty Thousand Japanese Yen (¥50,000) (the “Purchased Shares”);

    B.        iAccele and IAG have memorialized the Investment in that certain Investment Agreement between them dated as of even date herewith (the “Investment Agreement”).

    C.        Before giving effect to the Investment, InfoShowerX is the sole shareholder of iAccele. InfoShowerX will benefit from the Investment in that that ¥75,000,000 of the Investment will be used to pay InfoShowerX a portion of the total payment of ¥150,000,000 (the “InfoShowerX Payment”) due by iAccele to InfoShowerX for assets it transferred to iAccele in December 2002 to separately constitute iAccele as an independent business unit.

    D.        To induce IAG to enter into the Investment Agreement and to make the Investment, InfoShowerX desires to indemnify IAG from damages it may incur as a result of iAccele’s breach of the Investment Agreement or the inaccuracy of iAccele’s representations and warranties in the Investment Agreement.

    E.        InfoShowerX, iAccele and IAG desire to set forth in writing their agreements and understandings with respect to such indemnification and to matters ancillary thereto.

        NOW, THEREFORE, for and in consideration of the premises, covenants, and agreements contained herein, and other good and valuable consideration, the receipt and sufficiency of which is hereby acknowledged, the parties do covenant, agree, represent, warrant, and stipulate as follows:

A g r e e m e n t

1.     INDEMNIFICATION

(a)     Scope. InfoShowerX hereby unconditionally agree to indemnify and hold IAG harmless against, and will reimburse IAG on demand for, any payment, loss, cost, liability, demand, damage, and expense, including reasonable attorneys’ fees (including reasonable attorney’s fees and reasonable costs of investigation incurred in defending against such payment, loss, cost or expense or claim therefor) (“Losses”) made or incurred by or asserted against IAG at any time after the date hereof, in respect of any and all damage or deficiency resulting from any omission, misrepresentation, breach of warranty, or nonfulfillment of any term, provision, covenant or agreement on the part of iAccele contained in the Investment Agreement, or from any misrepresentation in, or omission from, any certificate or other instrument furnished to IAG pursuant to the Investment Agreement.

1.2.     Limitation on Indemnification Obligation. The obligations of InfoShowerX to indemnify IAG pursuant to this Agreement shall only be applicable to Losses arising within three (3) years from the date hereof.
