EXHIBIT 10.23.3

 

Restated Service Addendum

 

 

This is a Restated Service Addendum that replaces and restates the Service Addendum (Service Addendum No. 1) to the Service Agreement (the “Agreement”) between The Dow Chemical Company (“TDCC”) and Union Carbide Corporation (“UCC”), with an Effective Date of 6 February 2001, as amended.  TDCC and UCC agree that this Restated Service Addendum is applicable effective February 6, 2001.  Per Article 3 of the Agreement this Restated Service Addendum specifies services which TDCC (or its designated Affiliate) or UCC (or its designated Affiliates) are to perform pursuant to the terms and conditions of the Agreement.
