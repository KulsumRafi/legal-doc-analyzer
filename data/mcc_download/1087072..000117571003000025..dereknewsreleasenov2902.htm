Private Placement


(Vancouver, B.C., November 29, 2002) – Derek Resources Corporation has agreed to complete a private placement for 300,000 units at a price of $0.10 per unit.  Each unit consists of one common share and one common share purchase warrant exercisable at a price of $0.15 for a period of two years. All documentation and funds have been received by the Company.  Closing of the private placement is subject to regulatory approval.  The securities issued under this private placement will be subject to a four month hold period.  There were no commissions paid on this private placement.
