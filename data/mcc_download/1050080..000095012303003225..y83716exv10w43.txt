                                                                   Exhibit 10.43

March 7, 2003

Crescent Real Estate Equities and Vornado Realty Trust (the "Landlord")
acknowledge there will be a need to restructure the existing leases between
Landlord and AmeriCold Logistics LLC (the "Tenant"). As a first step towards a
larger lease restructure, the Landlord will extend the maturity of the current
rent deferral period from December 31, 2003 to December 31, 2004.
