                         MULTI-CURRENCY CREDIT AGREEMENT


         This MULTI-CURRENCY CREDIT AGREEMENT is made as of the 16th day of
January, 2003, by and among LIBERTY PROPERTY LIMITED PARTNERSHIP, a Pennsylvania
limited partnership (the "Borrower"), LIBERTY PROPERTY TRUST, a Maryland trust
(the "Company"), the Co-Borrowers named on the signature pages hereof and any
entity that may become an additional Co-Borrower pursuant to ss.5.6 (the
"Co-Borrowers") and FLEET NATIONAL BANK, a national banking association
("Fleet"), the other lending institutions which are listed from time to time on
Schedule 1 (collectively, with Fleet, the "Lenders"), and FLEET NATIONAL BANK,
as administrative agent for itself and such other lending institutions (the
"Agent").

         WHEREAS, the Borrower has requested that Fleet, the Lenders and certain
other lenders provide an unsecured revolving credit facility in the initial
maximum amount of $300,000,000 (the "Revolving Facility ") to the Borrower and
the Co-Borrowers and Fleet, the Lenders and certain other lenders have agreed to
provide such Facility subject to the terms and conditions set forth in a Credit
Agreement of even date herewith among the Borrower, the Co-Borrowers, the Agent,
the Lenders and certain other lenders (the "Revolving Facility Credit
Agreement");

         WHEREAS, the Borrower has requested that Fleet and the Lenders provide
an unsecured multi-currency revolving credit facility in the maximum amount of
$50,000,000 (the "Facility") to the Borrower and Fleet and the Lenders have
agreed to provide such Facility subject to the terms and conditions set forth
herein;

         NOW, THEREFORE, to accomplish these purposes, the Agent, the Borrower,
the Company and the Lenders hereby agree as follows:

forth in this ss.l or elsewhere in the provisions of this Agreement referred to
