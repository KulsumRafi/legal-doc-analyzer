executors, successors and assigns, jointly and severally, firmly by these

the Obligee, dated the 12TH day of JUNE, 2002, for ST87400047, ST8741000, AND
WS85500049, 2002 RESIDENTIAL STREET OVERLAY PROGRAM, for which contract is
hereby referred to and made a part hereof as fully and to the same extent as if
copied at length herein.

conditions, and agreements of any all duly authorized modifications of said
contract that may hereafter be made,notice of which modifications to the Surety

remain in full force and effect.

     PROVIDED, HOWEVER, that this bond is executed pursuant to the provisions
of Title 34, Chapter 2, Article 2, of the Arizona Revised Statutes, and all
