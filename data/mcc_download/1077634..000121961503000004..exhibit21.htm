INVESTMENT AGREEMENT

        THIS INVESTMENT AGREEMENT (the “Agreement”) is made as of February 10th, 2003, by and between iAccele Co., Ltd., a corporation organized and existing under the laws of Japan (the “Company”), and IA Global, Inc., a corporation organized and existing under the laws of the State of Delaware (the “Investor”).

P r e l i m i n a r y   S t a t e m e n t s

    A.        The Investor desires to invest One Hundred Million Japanese Yen (¥100,000,000) in the Company (the “Investment”), and to receive, in consideration therefor, Two Thousand (2,000) shares of the Company’s Common Stock, each with a face value of Fifty Thousand Japanese Yen (¥50,000) (the “Purchased Shares”);

    B.        The Company desires to receive such Investment from the Investor, and, in consideration therefor, to issue to the Investor the Purchased Shares; and

    C.        The Company and the Investor desire to set forth in writing their agreements and understandings with respect to the Investment and to matters ancillary thereto;

        NOW, THEREFORE, for and in consideration of the premises, covenants, and agreements contained herein, and other good and valuable consideration, the receipt and sufficiency of which is hereby acknowledged, the parties do covenant, agree, represent, warrant, and stipulate as follows:

A g r e e m e n t

1.     INVESTMENT

1.1.     Investment. Subject to the terms and conditions set forth herein, the Investor hereby subscribes for the Purchased Shares from the Company in exchange for the Investment in the amount of One Hundred Million Japanese Yen (¥100,000,000) (the “Purchase Price”), which amount shall be paid to the Company by wire transfer within five business days of the date hereof. Certificates evidencing the Purchased Shares shall be issued to the Investor as soon as practicable after the Company’s receipt of the Purchase Price.

1.2.     Use of Proceeds. The parties acknowledge and agree that ¥75,000,000 of the Purchase Price shall be used to pay InfoShowerX Co., Ltd. (“InfoShowerX”), the current parent company of the Company, a portion of the total payment of ¥150,000,000 (the “InfoShowerX Payment”) due by the Company to InfoShowerX for assets it transferred to the Company in December 2002 to separately constitute the Company as an independent business unit. The parties further agree that the balance of the Purchase Price shall be used by the Company for working capital purposes.

1.3.     Certain Acknowledgements. The Company and the Investor acknowledge that Inter Asset Japan LBO No. 1 Fund, a Japanese limited partnership (“IAJ”), which together with certain affiliated entities is the majority owner of the Investor, and certain entities affiliated with IAJ, own a significant equity position in InfoShowerX, and that InfoShowerX has certain debts outstanding to IAJ. A portion of the Purchase Price, used by the Company to make a portion of the InfoShowerX Payment, may be used by InfoShowerX to repay some or all of its debt to IAJ. The parties further acknowledge that the amount and the circumstances of the InfoShowerX Payment have been analyzed by Norihiko SATOH, Certified Public Accountant (CPA), and have been found to be regular and appropriate, as discussed in his Report on the Financial Conditions of i-Accele Corporation dated January 27, 2003 (a copy of which is attached hereto as Exhibit “A” hereto).

1.4.     Board Representation. The Company acknowledges and agrees that the issuance of the Purchased Shares to the Investor will entitle the Investor to elect three out of five directors on the Board of Directors of the Company, excluding audit committee directors. Promptly after the execution of this Agreement, the Company will take all necessary steps to increase the number of seats on its Board of Directors to five, and to permit the Investor to appoint three of such directors for election. In the event that the Board is increased to a number greater than five, the Investor will have the right to appoint a majority of the directors to serve on the Board.

1.5.     Stock Options Plan. The Company acknowledges and agrees that in the first quarter of fiscal year 2003 it will establish a incentive stock option plan for key management members with a commitment of shares of Common Stock of the Company equal to five to seven percent of the total issued and outstanding Common Stock.

1.6.     Indemnification Agreement. As a material inducement to the Investor to enter into this Agreement, InfoShowerX, the Company’s current majority shareholder, is entering into that certain Indemnification Agreement with the Investor, in the form attached hereto as Exhibit “B” (the “Indemnification Agreement”).
