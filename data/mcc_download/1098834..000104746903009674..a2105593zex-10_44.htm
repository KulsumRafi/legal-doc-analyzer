        WHEREAS, the Company, the Banks and the Agent are the parties to that certain Credit Agreement dated as of September 15, 2000, as amended to date (as so amended, the "Original Agreement");

        WHEREAS, the Company, the Banks and the Agent desire to amend and restate the Original Agreement in accordance with the terms hereof.
