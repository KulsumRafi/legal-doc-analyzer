Subject to the other terms of this Agreement, ImmunoGen retains the right to use the Licensed Technology and practice the Licensed Patent Rights and to use ImmunoGen's interest in all Improvements (i) to perform its work under this Agreement (ii) to develop, have developed, make, have made, use, have used, sell have sold, offer for sale, import, have imported, export and have exported any product that is not a Licensed Product and (iii) for any and all uses outside of the Field.
