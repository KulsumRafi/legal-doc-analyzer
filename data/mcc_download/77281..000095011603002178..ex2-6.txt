                         AGREEMENT OF PURCHASE AND SALE

                                      EXTON

         THIS AGREEMENT OF PURCHASE AND SALE ("Agreement") is made and entered
into as of the 7th day of March, 2003, (the "Effective Date") by and between The
Rouse Company, L.P., a Delaware limited partnership ("Seller"), and PR EXTON
LIMITED PARTNERSHIP, a Pennsylvania limited partnership ("Buyer").

                              W I T N E S S E T H:

         WHEREAS, as of Closing, Seller will own directly or indirectly all of
the membership interests in two (2) Delaware limited liability companies
(collectively, the "Direct Transfer Entities"), which will own, directly or
indirectly through the Intermediate Entities identified on Schedule 1.1A, all of
the membership interests in Exton Square Property, LLC, a Delaware limited
liability company ("Owner Entity" and, together with the Direct Transfer
Entities and the Intermediate Entities, the "Transferred Entities"); and Owner
Entity is the owner of certain real property located in Exton, Pennsylvania and
known as "Exton Square Mall";

         WHEREAS, Whiteland Holding Limited Partnership, a Maryland limited
partnership wholly owned (indirectly through intermediaries) by Seller (the
"Ground Lessee"), is the owner of (i) certain real property located in Exton,
Pennsylvania, adjacent to Exton Square Mall, as more fully described in Exhibit
A-3 (the "Adjacent Land"), and all improvements located thereon (the "Adjacent
Improvements" and, together with the Adjacent Land, the "Adjacent Property"),
and (ii) a leasehold interest in land adjacent to Exton Square Mall (the "Leased
Land"), and all improvements located thereon (the "Ground Lease Improvements"),
as described in Exhibit A-2, pursuant to the ground lease (the "Ground Lease")
described on Exhibit F; and

         WHEREAS, Seller desires to (a) sell, convey, transfer and assign to
Buyer, and Buyer desires to purchase and acquire from Seller, all of the
ownership interests in the Direct Transfer Entities pursuant to the terms hereof
and (b) cause the Ground Lessee to (i) sell, convey, transfer and assign to
Buyer, and Buyer desires to purchase and acquire from Seller, the Adjacent
Property, and (ii) transfer and assign all of Ground Lessee's right, title and
interest in the Ground Lease to Buyer.

         NOW, THEREFORE, in consideration of the mutual covenants contained
herein, and for good and valuable consideration, the receipt and sufficiency of
which are hereby acknowledged, Seller and Buyer agree as follows:
