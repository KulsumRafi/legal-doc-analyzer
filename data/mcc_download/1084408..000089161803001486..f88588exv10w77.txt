PAYMENTS

Actual bonus payments will be determined by measurement of individual
performance as determined by the CEO. The CEO shall be responsible for setting
recommendations and presenting to the Compensation Committee for approval.

ELIGIBILITY

In order to be eligible to receive a bonus, the individual must be employed by
Veritas at the time of payment.

Notwithstanding the above, the Company's CEO and/or Compensation Committee of

modify or change this Plan or its implementation at any time.
